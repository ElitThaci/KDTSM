KCAA Drone Flight Regulations

By using this system, you agree to comply with all Kosovo Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA) regulations governing unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) operations.

General Requirements

  • Maximum flight altitude: 120 meters Above Ground Level (AGL)
  • Visual Line of Sight (VLOS) must be maintained at all times
  • Daylight operations only (06:00 - 20:00 local time)
  • Minimum visibility: 3,000 meters
  • Maximum wind speed for operations: 40 km/h

Restricted Zones

  • No flights within 8km of Prishtina International Airport
  • No flights within 5km of Gjakova Airport
  • No flights over military installations, government buildings, or diplomatic facilities
  • No flights over critical infrastructure

Pilot Requirements

  • Minimum age: 16 years
  • Drone registration required
  • Third-party liability insurance mandatory
  • Completion of safety training recommended

Penalties

Violation of these regulations may result in fines, confiscation of equipment, and/or criminal prosecution under Kosovo aviation law.
